Office of Buildings

The Office of Buildings reviews, approves and issues all permits required by the City's Codes for the repair, alteration or addition to all public and private buildings and structures; as well as, permits for new construction. The Office also assists property owners in their effort to ensure that buildings are in compliance with the standards set forth in the City's Codes.
